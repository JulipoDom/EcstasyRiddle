This riddle Is considered an ARG(Alternative Reality Game), in other words It takes the riddles from outside of the website, some tools will be necessary, like: photoshop audacity, and others. All the resources can and must be used. In each riddle there will be a page with tips where you can choose from a basic tip, an essential tip and the riddle resolution. There will be 5 riddles and at the end of the game you will receive an congratulation screen for have passed at all the riddles. I wish you luck on this journey.
